George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology is a public college in Toronto, Ontario.
- The college offers a broad range of educational programs:
certificates, diplomas, advanced diplomas, degrees, postgraduate certificates,
apprenticeships, and continuing education.
It is focused heavily on experiential learning (hands-on, real-world training), industry partnerships,
community engagement.

Campuses & Locations
George Brown College has several key campuses and locations, mostly in downtown Toronto:
Casa Loma Campus — 160 Kendal Avenue
St. James Campus — 200 King Street East, among other nearby buildings like 300 Adelaide, etc.
Waterfront Campus — Dockside Drive and expansions including “Limberlost Place” etc.
